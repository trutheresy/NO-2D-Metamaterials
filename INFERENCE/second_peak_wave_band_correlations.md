# Second-peak wavevector and band correlations

Analysis of per-sample losses on I3O5 inference (group channel weighting: 50% eigenfrequency ch0 + 50% mean displacement ch1–4; `nmae_eps = nmse_eps = 1e-5`).

The **second peak** is the high-loss mode in log-scaled NMAE histograms. A sample is in the second peak when its NMAE exceeds the log-space valley between the two fitted modes.

## Wavevectors with >50% second-peak membership
